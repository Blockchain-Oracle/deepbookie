# What is DeepBookie?

DeepBookie is a chat app for trading on Sui. You type what you want in plain
English — *"Bet $50 that Bitcoin is above $70,000 on Friday"* — and an AI prices
it, shows you the cost and the payout, and builds the transaction. You sign it in
your own wallet.

**The AI proposes. You sign. It never holds your keys or touches your money.**

## Two markets, one chat

DeepBookie trades two DeepBook markets on Sui testnet:

- **Predict** — yes/no bets on a price. *"Will BTC be above $70k on Friday?"* If
  you're right, you win a fixed payout.
- **Spot** — ordinary trading on an order book. Swap one coin for another, or
  place a limit order.

You don't pick a market. You just say what you want, and the AI uses the right
tools.

## Four ways to use it

The same tools work everywhere:

- **Web app** — the chat in your browser; sign with your wallet.
- **MCP server** — run the tools inside Claude Desktop, Cursor, or Claude Code.
- **CLI** — trade from your terminal.
- **Claude skill** — a playbook that teaches any Claude agent how to trade here.
