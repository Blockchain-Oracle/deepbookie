## How we pick what to build next

Every milestone here was picked by one rule: **does it help more people trade DeepBook?** Mainnet, mobile, voice, more assets — every one removes a step between *"I want to make this trade"* and *"my wallet signed it."*

If something you want isn't on this list, [open an issue](https://github.com/Blockchain-Oracle/deepbookie/issues) — the roadmap is public on purpose.
