# Roadmap

What's live today, what's being built, and where DeepBookie is headed.

## Now — live on Sui testnet

- **Live web app** — chat to trade DeepBook at [deepbookie.xyz](https://deepbookie.xyz). Predict and Spot, signed in your wallet.
- **44 tools** — the full DeepBook surface: Predict (yes/no BTC markets) and Spot (the order book).
- **5 npm packages** — `predict-client`, `core`, `node`, `mcp`, and `cli`, all under `@deepbookie`.
- **Claude skill** — install the agent in one line.
- **MCP server + CLI** — trade from Claude Desktop, Cursor, or your terminal.
- **First Predict TypeScript client** — no one had shipped one for DeepBook Predict.

## Next — building now

- **Mainnet** — move from testnet to real trades on Sui mainnet.
- **Mobile app** — iOS and Android. Chat to trade from your phone.
- **Telegram & Discord bot** — trade without leaving your group chat.

## Later — on the roadmap

- **Voice trading** — talk to DeepBookie instead of typing. Same wallet signature at the end.
- **More markets** — ETH, SOL, and beyond.
- **Agent strategies** — auto-trade within limits you set: stop-loss, take-profit, scheduled bets.
- **Embeddable SDK** — drop DeepBookie's chat into any Sui dapp in a few lines.

---

Every item here follows one rule: **does it help more people trade DeepBook?** If something you want isn't on the list, [open an issue](https://github.com/Blockchain-Oracle/deepbookie/issues) — the roadmap is public on purpose.
